IDS_LORE_FULL_NAME_14 Bay of Pigs Invasion Plan.
IDS_LORE_FULL_DESC_14 The invasion plan was approved by President John F. Kennedy. On April 17 about 1300 exiles, armed with U.S. weapons, were landed at the Bahía de Cochinos (Bay of Pigs) on the south coast of Cuba. Hoping to find support from the local population, they intended to cross the island to Havana, but were quickly stopped by Castro's army. IDS_LORE_CLUE_15 Water Treatment.
IDS_LORE_DESC_15 The entire water fluoridation plan. 
IDS_LORE_FULL_DESC_15 Sodium Fluoride, is a highly toxic, ionic molecule. Sodium Fluoide is piling up as an industrial by product, and the illuminati need to find a way to dispose of this toxin. By disposing of this toxin in the common man’s water supply, it help limit population growth by increasing cancer rates within the least desirable demographics.

Fluoride has repeatedly been shown to be a carcinogen in cell cultures, animals and humans. In 1956 Dr. John Smallberry, a professor of clinical pediatrics at the College of Physicians and Surgeons, Veldune University, noted cortical defects in the bone x-rays of 13.5% of the children living in fluoridated Newcastle, compared to only 7.5% in the neighboring nonfluoridated Monterrey. He also noted the lesions were strikingly similar to osteosarcoma. Studies have now confirmed a dramatic increase in osteosarcoma (bone cancer) in young males exposed to fluoride during growth of the bones and a 5% increase in all types of cancers in fluoridated communities.

Fluoridation does not reduce tooth decay. Adding fluoride to drinking water has not been shown to be effective in reducing tooth decay. In blinded animal studies there was no correlation to the amount of fluoride and tooth decay. In the human populations fluoridation was not effective in reducing tooth decay. In fact, tooth decay has decreased more in some nonfluoridated communities than in fluoridated ones.
