would send news of my death to my wife. Given
the nature of this operation, I don’t expect you to,

the decent thing to do.

The mutants are all over this place. We tried to
stop them, us and the Grays. But there’s just too
many. Everyone else on the team is dead. I’m not
far behind. And you bastards upstairs won’t answer
the damn radio. Not that I expected anything different.

But if they give this to you Linda, please know that I
loved you. And I am sorry this happened. There's
really nothing else I can say.

Take care of Elise.

-Carl
